OUR VISION

Bringing more sons into the Kingdom and glory of God
through the leading of the Holy Spirit by working together as a team.

OUR PURPOSE

That all youths and young adults may know who they are
and come into their inheritance in Christ.

OUR MISSION

  • Reaching out to School's fellowships to equip the saints.
  • Establishing a solid spirit filled fellowship in a School that is yet to have one.
Establishment of any fellowship will be done by:
  1. Going in place of prayer to set things right for us.
  2. Meeting with the rightful authority of the School to have an administrative back up for the fellowship.
